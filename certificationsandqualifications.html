Certifications

  • Nuclear Regulatory Commission (NRC) License - Required in the U.S. to operate nuclear facilities.
  • ISO 9001 - Quality management systems certification.
  • ISO 14001 - Environmental management systems certification.
  • ISO 45001 - Occupational health and safety management systems certification.
  • ASME Nuclear Quality Assurance (NQA-1) - Standard for quality assurance in nuclear facilities.
  • ISO/IEC 17025 - General requirements for the competence of testing and calibration laboratories.
  • Nuclear Energy Agency (NEA) Membership - Participation in international nuclear safety and security discussions.
  • American National Standards Institute (ANSI) Certification - Compliance with various standards for safety and performance.
  • Quality Assurance Program (QAP) - Specific to nuclear reactor components and systems.
  • Nuclear Security Regulations Compliance - Adherence to physical and cybersecurity standards.
  • Radiation Safety Certification - Compliance with safety standards for radiation exposure.
  • Engineering and Manufacturing Certifications - Such as ISO/TS 16949 for automotive applications, applicable to specific components.
  • Fukushima Response Certification - Demonstrating compliance with enhanced safety measures post-Fukushima.
  • IAEA Safeguards Certification - Compliance with International Atomic Energy Agency safeguards.
